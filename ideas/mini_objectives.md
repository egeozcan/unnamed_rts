## 18. Mini-Objectives & Side Missions

**Idea**: Dynamic triggers during a match that provide rewards (credits, units, map reveals).

**Implementation Direction**:
- **Triggers**: Game time, building milestones, or neutral entities (e.g., escorting a convoy).
- **System**: An objective manager tracking active tasks and rewards.

**Hardness**: 6/10 | **Complexity**: 7/10
