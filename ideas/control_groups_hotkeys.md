## 14. Control Groups & Hotkeys

**Idea**: Standard RTS selection management (Ctrl+1-9 to group, 1-9 to select).

**Implementation Direction**:
- **Logic**: Client-side mapping of IDs to numbers. Double-tap to center camera.
- **Maintenance**: Automatically remove units from groups upon death.
- **Priority**: Essential Quality-of-Life feature for competitive play.

**Hardness**: 2/10 | **Complexity**: 3/10
