## 20. Environmental Hazards & Map Events

**Idea**: Dangerous terrain or periodic events that affect the whole map.

**Implementation Direction**:
- **Hazards**: Damaging terrain (e.g., Tiberium), Sandstorms (reduced vision), Ion Storms (disables air).
- **Interactive**: Destructible bridges, explosive oil barrels.

**Hardness**: 5/10 | **Complexity**: 6/10
