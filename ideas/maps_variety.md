## 7. Maps & Generator Variety

**Idea**: Expansion of map content via premade layouts and enhanced procedural generation.

**7a. Premade Maps**:
- **Format**: JSON-based terrain grids, resource placements, and starting positions.
- **Editor**: Potential for a simple in-game editor for community content.

**7b. Generator Improvements**:
- **Symmetry**: For fairness, generate one quadrant/half and mirror it (rotational or mirror symmetry). Add slight noise for a natural feel.
- **Themes**: Desert (open), Forest (rocks/cover), Islands (water), Mountains (choke points).
- **New Terrain**: Water (impassable/amphibious), Cliffs, different ore types.

**Hardness**: 5/10 | **Complexity**: 6/10
